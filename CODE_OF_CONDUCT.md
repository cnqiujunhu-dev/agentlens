# AgentLens Code of Conduct

AgentLens is built for people debugging AI systems together. Keep the project useful, direct, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear, technical, and constructive.
- Assume traces may contain sensitive data and help people redact or minimize examples.
- Critique code, design, docs, and claims without attacking people.
- Stay on topic in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Make room for maintainers to ask for smaller reproductions or narrower changes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private prompts, customer data, credentials, or sensitive traces without permission.
- Repeated spam, off-topic promotion, or hostile argument after a maintainer asks to stop.
- Impersonation or misrepresentation of affiliation with the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or delete comments and issues that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

If a conduct issue includes a security vulnerability or sensitive trace data, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) and avoid public disclosure.
